About My Audio to Text

My Audio to Text is a free web tool designed to convert spoken language into text in real-time, directly within your browser. Utilizing the Web Speech API (SpeechRecognition), it offers instant transcription for various languages, including English, Portuguese, Spanish, and Bahasa Indonesia. As a Progressive Web App (PWA), it provides offline support and can be installed on your devices for convenient access.

Our mission is to provide an accessible, user-friendly, and efficient solution for converting audio to text, catering to a wide range of needs from quick notes to detailed transcriptions. We are committed to continuous improvement and expanding our language support and features.
